About Board of Sports Activities

The Sports at IIT Ropar is an integral part of the education process. The students have various sports facilities which they enjoy every day in campus. Presently, IIT Ropar has one cricket field, three cricket practise pitches, three clay tennis courts, two volleyball courts, a football field, a hockey field, a gym, several table tennis tables, a basketball court, a badminton court and much more for athletic events.

All the sports facilities have flood light arrangements to play after sunset. In all these sports activities the students practice and enjoy the sports activities during leisure time in morning and evening.The institute also encourages students to participate in Inter-IIT sport events and other competitions. The appointment of a coach has further augmented sports-related activities in the campus

Aarohan

From the year 2016, IIT Ropar has started celebrating Sports Day AAROHAN on an annual basis. The cheif guest for this event was former Team India Hockey captain and Olympian Gold medalist,Arjuna Awardee Shri Surinder Singh Sodhi, IPS.

Various Sports At IIT Ropar

Games and sports are not only important for success in studio but it is important for success in every walks of our life. Games and sports include all outdoor and indoor games and also athletics.

Inter IIT Sports Meet is the annual sports tournament of the Indian Institutes of Technology. It is organized in December every year, with the Aquatics events held separately in October. The seven old IITs take turns hosting the event. Inter IIT Sports Meet is the only event wherein all the IITs participate.
